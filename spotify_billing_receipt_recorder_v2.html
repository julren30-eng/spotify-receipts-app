Save this app to your phone — no internet needed
All your data is saved in your browser's local storage — it stays even offline. Follow these steps to install it like an app on your home screen.
On Android (Chrome)
  • 1
    Open this page in Chrome
    Make sure you're on the Claude.ai page with this tool open
  • 2
    Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) at the top right
    Then tap "Add to Home screen"
  • 3
    Tap "Add" on the confirmation prompt
    The icon will appear on your home screen like a real app
  • 4
    Open the app from your home screen
    Works fully offline — your receipts are stored on your device
On iPhone (Safari)
  • 1
    Open this page in Safari (not Chrome)
    PWA install only works in Safari on iOS
  • 2
    Tap the Share button at the bottom
    It looks like a box with an arrow pointing up
  • 3
    Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
    Give it a name like "Spotify Receipts"
  • 4
    Tap "Add" in the top right
    Launch it from your home screen anytime — no internet required
Export and backup your data
  • 1
    Tap "Export data" below to download a JSON backup
    Save it to Google Drive or your Files app for safekeeping
  • 2
    To restore, tap "Import data" and select your backup file
    All receipts and advance payments will be restored
Tips for offline use
  • ✓
    All data saved automatically on every entry
    No "Save" button needed — just add and close
  • ✓
    Use GCash or Maya as your payment method
    Log the payment here right after sending money
  • ✓
    Export a backup monthly
    Protects against clearing browser data accidentally
  • ✓
    Check advance billing tab before collecting
    See who still has months left so you don't double-charge
